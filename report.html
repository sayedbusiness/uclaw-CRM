The six critical defects

  1. C1 Schema broadcasts the wrong address & phone

    Their structured data tells Google "3400 Watt Ave / (916) 573-0474" while the real office is 3301 Watt Ave / (916) 800-8457 — NAP poisoning that suppresses map rankings daily. Citations are already split between the two addresses.

  2. C2 Homepage has zero H1 tags

    The single most important on-page element doesn't exist on the most important page — topic is inferred from a slider graphic.

  3. C3 24 of 24 images missing alt text

    100% failure: dead image SEO, WCAG violation, and ADA-lawsuit exposure — on a law firm's website.

  4. C4 No pixel, no call tracking, no chat, no retargeting

    GTM/GA4 only. Phone calls (the primary conversion) are invisible; the 97% of visitors who don't convert are unreachable forever.

  5. C5 Content footprint at 1/3 of market requirement

    4 practice pages vs competitors' 12+. No truck, motorcycle, pedestrian, wrongful-death, or TBI pages — each a money keyword they can't rank for. Dedicated service pages are the #1 local organic factor (Whitespark 2026).

  6. C6 The multilingual moat is invisible to search

    Five languages — including Dari/Farsi in a city with one of America's largest Afghan communities — with zero localized pages, zero hreflang. An uncontested lane nobody in the market occupies.

The asymmetric strategy

Don't out-mass Ashton & Price or Demas on head terms day one. Win the lanes the giants can't enter:

Tier 1 Demand capture

Google LSA (position 0, pay-per-lead, powered by the review engine) · tight exact-match Search with weekly negatives · GBP + Bing Places (feeds ChatGPT — 15.9% conversion vs 1.76% organic) + Apple Business Connect.

Tier 2 Compounding assets

40+ page hub-and-spoke rebuild · 18-day-rule review engine · multilingual moat (/es/, Dari, Arabic + hreflang) · AEO: FAQ schema, answer-first copy, Yelp/BBB/Reddit surfaces, "best of" list placements.

Tier 3 Demand creation

Founder-story UGC video (Angle 22 — "In 2017 I was the one in the hospital bed") on Meta broad + CAPI · sequenced retargeting with real statute-of-limitations urgency · bilingual YouTube/TikTok education.

The automation system — 32 workers, four jobs

Think of it as a tireless staff member who answers every lead in under a minute (in five languages), keeps clients happy and reviewing, runs the marketing, and guards every legal deadline.
